Hero section: "Austin-Area Homeowners — File Your Insurance Claim With Confidence" + strong CTA
Trust signals: licensed/insured badges, Texas contractor info, before/after photos

Phase 2 — Wire Up Lead Delivery (Same Day)

Two fast options to get leads to him instantly:

Formspree or EmailJS — form submissions go straight to his email/SMS, zero backend needed
Zapier — connect the form to a Google Sheet and send him a text via Twilio every time someone submits
Phase 3 — Deploy (Same Day)

Host on Netlify (free, instant deployment, custom domain support)
Point a domain like ecdesignbuilds.com or austininsuranceclaims.com to it
Phase 4 — Drive Traffic (Week 1)

Set up a Google Business Profile if he doesn't have one (huge for local SEO)
Run a small Google Ads campaign targeting "roof damage claim Austin" / "storm damage contractor Austin"
Add the site link to his existing social profiles
